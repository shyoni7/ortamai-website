## Design System

### Color Palette (Futuristic, Modern, Elegant)
- **Primary Dark**: Deep Space Blue/Black (#0a0e27 or similar)
- **Accent 1**: Electric Turquoise (#00d9ff or similar)
- **Accent 2**: Futuristic Purple (#8b5cf6 or similar)
- **Text Light**: Off-white/Light Gray (#f0f4f8)
- **Text Dark**: Dark Slate (#1a1f3a)

### Typography
- **Display Font**: Bold, modern sans-serif (e.g., Space Grotesk, Sora)
- **Body Font**: Clean, readable sans-serif (e.g., Inter, Heebo for Hebrew)
- **Accent Font**: Geometric sans-serif for highlights

### Visual Elements
- Glassmorphism effects (frosted glass overlays)
- Grid patterns and data flow visualizations
- Subtle glow effects on interactive elements
- Smooth animations and transitions
- RTL support for Hebrew content
